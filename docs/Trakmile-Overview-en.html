About Trakmile

What is Trakmile?

Trakmile is a logistics operating system that empowers delivery companies and third-party logistics providers (3PL) to manage their entire operations from a single platform — from order intake to proof of delivery.

Vision

Enable the logistics sector in the Middle East to achieve full digital transformation in line with Saudi Vision 2030.

Mission

Deliver intelligent logistics solutions that reduce cost, increase efficiency, and improve the end-customer experience.

Target Audience

Delivery companies, 3PL providers, and distribution centers — across all sizes and operational levels.

Regional Coverage

Purpose-built for the Middle East with full Arabic support and local operations (COD, SMS, and more).

Company Profile · Accounting
Accounting

Built-in Accounting System

An accounting system built into the platform — no separate software needed. Every delivery or collection operation is automatically reflected in financial records.

Financial Dashboard

Comprehensive view of revenue, expenses, collections, and KPIs — in real time from one place.

Chart of Accounts

Manage customer, driver, and vendor accounts with updated balances and detailed statements.

Invoices

Issue invoices linked to shipments and delivery services, with payment status tracking.

Receipts & Payments

Record cash collections and transfers, and disburse driver and vendor payments.

Expenses

Record and track operating expenses — salaries, fuel, maintenance — with proper accounting classification.

Settlements

Calculate COD, commissions, and deductions, and close each driver's account periodically.

Financial Reports

Revenue, expense, and profit reports, COD, receivables, and driver payables — exportable.

Configure currency, payment methods, billing policies, and financial data access permissions.
